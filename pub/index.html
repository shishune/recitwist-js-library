ReciTwist.js

 Library!

Cooking is more of an art rather than a science. There are many substitutes that can be made that can make a recipe more interesting, and this sort of information should be available to the user.

ReciTwist is a library that allows developers to easily show alternative ingredients in their recipes, allowing users to actively engage with their website by visually swapping the ingredients, facilitates formatting each step in the recipe, and creates a corresponding timeline to visualize the time breakdown of each step.
